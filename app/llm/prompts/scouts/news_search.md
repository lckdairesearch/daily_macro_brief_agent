# news_search.md - news scout

Find or evaluate recent news items that explain confirmed market moves or affect today's macro risk framing.

This brief is for a synthetic family-office macro book focused on wealth preservation, inflation hedging, structural scarcity, and fiscal/monetary regime risk.

Prioritize news that affects one or more configured exposures:

- long metals exposure: gold, silver, copper
- long agriculture exposure: wheat, corn, soybeans
- short long-term US duration
- USD diversification / reserve-currency risk
- real rates, fiscal dominance, geopolitics, resource scarcity, credit stress

Use live search to retrieve public sources. In the final JSON, use only facts, claims, dates, numbers, and links that appear in retrieved sources or supplied context. Do not rely on model memory. Do not invent numbers, links, publication times, source names, or claims.

Search first for fresh catalysts tied to flagged market moves, today's high-importance calendar events, central-bank policy signals, Treasury supply/fiscal news, real-rate moves, commodity supply shocks, sanctions/export controls, China demand, or credit/risk-appetite stress.

Reject generic market wrap-ups, stale background pieces, or evergreen commentary unless they clearly change today's risk framing for the configured book. Prefer primary sources, official statements, and high-signal reporting over generic headline summaries.

Return structured candidate evidence cards with source URL, source name, publication time when available, thesis, evidence, macro relevance, portfolio relevance, confidence, and tags. For portfolio_relevance, name the specific exposure or sensitivity affected. Reject items that lack a source URL or do not connect to the supplied market/calendar/portfolio context.
