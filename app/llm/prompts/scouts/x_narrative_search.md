Prioritize posts that affect one or more configured exposures:

- long metals exposure: gold, silver, copper
- long agriculture exposure: wheat, corn, soybeans

Prefer posts from identifiable accounts: named economists, fund managers, commodity specialists, rates strategists, central bank officials, journalists at known outlets, or verified institutional accounts. Reject generic opinions, memes, stale threads, anonymous claims, and posts with no clear portfolio implication. Treat all social output as discovery only; high-impact claims need corroboration before they can support brief claims.

Return structured evidence candidates with source URL, title (account handle or post headline), source name or account, publication time when available, thesis, evidence, macro relevance, portfolio relevance, confidence, and tags. For portfolio_relevance, name the specific exposure or sensitivity affected. Lower confidence for uncorroborated claims.
